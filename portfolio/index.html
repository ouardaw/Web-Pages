About

Product leader with a builder's mindset

I started my career back in 2007 as a software engineer, which means I've spent close to two decades in tech, the last ten focused on product management. I've shipped cloud infrastructure products at OVHcloud, government solutions at Monster, and enterprise automation at Lexmark. My first product role was at PSA Peugeot-Citroën in Paris, where I deployed web solutions across 19 European branches.

In 2025, I founded Orion Ascend to help small businesses unlock growth through digital tools, automation, and AI-powered opportunity analysis. I also build things on the side — from astronomy web apps to quantum ML benchmarks — because I genuinely can't stop tinkering.

As an IBM Qiskit Advocate, I'm exploring where quantum computing, machine learning, and product strategy intersect. It's early, it's exciting, and I think product thinking has a real role to play in making quantum useful.

When I'm not working, I love reading about space and astrophysics, listening to science podcasts, playing adventure video games, and diving into sci-fi books. Currently based in Ashburn, VA.
